Investigation Overview¶

In this investigation, I wanted to look at most popular genre from year to year, follow by director with highest movie popularity and year of production and finally, movie production company that has the highest revenue and the corresponding year.

Dataset Overview¶

This dataset includes information about Internet Movie Database (IMDB) movie. The data consists of information regarding 10,000 movies collected from The Movie Database (IMDb) including user ratings and revenue.

Most popular Genre from year to year¶

We have total of 4 genres with the highest popularity from year to year with same popularity value of 32.985763. The 4 genres include Action, Adventure, Science fiction and Thriller

The director who has the highest popular movie was Colin Trevorrow with popularity rating of 32.985763. The movie was released in the year 2015, with original title 'Jurassic World'
